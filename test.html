SAVE THE WORLD

Around the world, too many children still start a life at a disadvantage because of who they are and where they come from.
Our concern is how the children around Kenya caught in the middle of conflicts.
Climate change, COVID-19 and conflicts in homes have left children vulnerable to critical levels of hunger and poverty.Those in shelters and homes, in prisons and hospital beds have families that forgot about them and we all can do something about it.
Help us protect the world by ensuring they not only survive but smile, thrive and become the best versions of themselves.
